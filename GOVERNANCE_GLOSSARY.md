# Governance Glossary

| Term | Institutional Definition |
|---|---|
| **Passive Stewardship** | A low-velocity operational phase focused entirely on continuity, security, and dependency management. No new features are merged. |
| **Review Council** | The federated group of maintainers responsible for validating compatibility and continuity. |
| **Survivability** | Ensuring the ecosystem can be maintained with minimal human intervention. |

*Note: Active intervention is reserved solely for critical CVEs and infrastructure rot.*
