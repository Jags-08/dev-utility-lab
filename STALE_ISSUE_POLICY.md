# Stale Issue Lifecycle Rules

## Policy
To maintain ecosystem clarity and minimize review fatigue, issues lacking activity for 90 days will be marked stale. After an additional 30 days without activity, they will be archived.

## Exceptions
- Core architectural refactors.
- Institutional continuity proposals.
- Long-term operational observability tasks.
## Reviewer Offboarding Exceptions

If a reviewer proposes an issue but is subsequently offboarded, the issue lifecycle remains active until audited by the council, subject to a hard 180-day limit before automatic archival.
